About this calculator

Tip amount is simply Bill × (Tip % ÷ 100), and the total is the bill plus that tip.

When splitting between several people, both the tip and the total are divided evenly — this assumes everyone is splitting equally. For itemized splits where people ordered different amounts, see the Split Bill calculator.
