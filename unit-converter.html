About this calculator

Length, weight and volume conversions all work the same way: your value is converted to a common base unit (metres, kilograms or litres) using each unit's conversion factor, then converted from that base unit into your target unit.

Temperature is different — it needs a formula rather than a simple multiplication, since 0° doesn't mean “nothing” the way 0kg does, so this calculator converts via Celsius as an intermediate step for Fahrenheit and Kelvin.
