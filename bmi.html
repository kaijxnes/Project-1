About this calculator

BMI is calculated as weight (kg) ÷ height (m)², or 703 × weight (lb) ÷ height (in)² in imperial units.

It's a quick screening tool, not a diagnosis — it doesn't distinguish muscle from fat, so very muscular people can show a higher BMI without carrying excess body fat. The standard categories are under 18.5 (underweight), 18.5–24.9 (normal), 25–29.9 (overweight), and 30+ (obese).
