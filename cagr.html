About this calculator

Compound Annual Growth Rate answers “what constant yearly growth rate would take my start value to my end value?” It's calculated as (End Value ÷ Start Value)^(1÷Years) − 1, expressed as a percentage.

Unlike a simple average of yearly changes, CAGR accounts for compounding, so it's the standard way to compare investment or business growth over multi-year periods.
