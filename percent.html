About this calculator

Percentage questions come in three common forms, each handled by its own tab.

“X% of Y” multiplies Y by X÷100. “X is what % of Y” divides X by Y and multiplies by 100. “% change” calculates (New − Old) ÷ Old × 100, and shows whether it's an increase or a decrease.
