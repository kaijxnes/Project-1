About this calculator

A single discount reduces the price by Price × (Discount % ÷ 100).

Stacked discounts are applied sequentially to whatever the price has become after the previous discount, not all at once on the original price — so two 10% discounts save 19%, not 20%, because the second 10% is taken off an already-reduced price.
