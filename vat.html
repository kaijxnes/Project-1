About this calculator

VAT is added to a net price using Net × (1 + VAT Rate), or removed from a gross price using Gross ÷ (1 + VAT Rate).

The income tax calculator applies each band's rate only to the portion of income that falls within it — a progressive system where earning more never reduces your take-home pay, since higher rates only apply to the slice above each threshold.
