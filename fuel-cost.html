About this calculator

In MPG mode, fuel needed is Distance ÷ MPG, multiplied by the price per gallon for total cost. In L/100km mode, fuel needed is (Distance ÷ 100) × the L/100km figure, multiplied by price per litre.

The two efficiency measures work in opposite directions — a higher MPG means better efficiency, but a lower L/100km does.
