About this calculator

Time zone offsets aren't fixed — many places move their clocks forward or back for daylight saving at different times of year, so a simple fixed offset would be wrong for half the year.

This calculator works out each time zone's actual offset for the specific date and time you enter, so daylight saving is handled automatically rather than needing a manual adjustment.
