About this calculator

Flooring area is simply Length × Width, with a waste percentage added on top to cover offcuts.

Paint coverage starts from the total wall area (2 × (Length + Width) × Height), subtracts a standard allowance for doors and windows, then divides by your paint's coverage rate per litre, multiplied by however many coats you're applying.
