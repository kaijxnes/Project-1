About this calculator

A single score is converted to a percentage (Score ÷ Total × 100) and matched against editable grade boundaries.

The weighted average multiplies each assignment's percentage by its weight, sums those, and divides by the total weight used — so a 90% test worth 50% of your grade counts twice as much as a 90% homework worth 25%.
